About me

Mayuri Kakkad

EXPERT MAKEUP & MEHENDI ARTIST

I am cosmetologist and licensed makeup artist with experience of 27 years. ​I am currently providing make up and henna services throughout Western Pennsylvania and am open to going around the Northeast for my services.

Life

I was born and raised in Hyderabad, India. Apart from work, I’m a classical dancer and enjoy music. I’m also a home-maker and take pride in raising my happy family with my husband and our two boys.

Experience

I have been a makeup and Mehndi artist for 27 years now. I take pride in adorning brides and clients with stunning designs that bring out their inner beauty and express their unique personalities. I have always been committed to providing artistic and professional services to make my clients’ experience with makeup and Mehndi unforgettable.

I believe in using only high-quality, safe products and take the utmost care in ensuring my clients’ safety and satisfaction. My henna paste is made with 100% natural Henna powder, lemon juice, and essential oils.
